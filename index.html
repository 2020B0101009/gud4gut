Meet the Founder

I am a mother, a wife, a daughter, a sister, and above all, A WOMAN. Ever since evolution, women have been the nurturers of the family, and food is the center point of the home. Being a woman I take up this responsibility to spread love and happiness across the world through food. Everyone wants a taste, whatever is in it. Then, why not health?

Empowering women to nourish their families with love and health, one meal at a time.
